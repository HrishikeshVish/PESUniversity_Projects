Lebron Lost his Magic in Lakers?
❮ ❯ 




	It seems appropriate that LeBron James would look like a magician tonight. His Los Angeles Lakers are, after all, playing against 
the Orlando Magic, yet it is James who is pulling off magnificent tricks. Take a look at this bounce pass. With three Magic 
defenders crowding him, he has no lane through the air. So he hits the floor to get JaVale McGee an easy bucket at the rim.


The Lakers are on an absolute roll right now. They are undefeated since signing Tyson Chandler, and added their most impressive victory
 yet when they beat the Portland Trail Blazers for the second time. There is no doubting the level of competition anymore, but now 
the Lakers have a different challenge in their first East Coast trip. They begin tonight in Orlando, and then have two very emotional 
games for LeBron James: at the Miami Heat and at the Cleveland Cavaliers. The Lakers have proven that they can win against typical 
teams under typical circumstances. They now need to prove that they can win with more on their minds than just basketball. This Magic 
game could have easily been a trap game with the Heat and Cavs looming.


But so far tonight, that hasn't been the case. The Lakers have controlled their matchup with the Magic from the start. It goes to show 
that no matter what conditions they are playing under, the Lakers will be just fine with James on the court.
